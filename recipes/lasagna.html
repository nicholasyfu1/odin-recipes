Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 3 (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Cook lasagna noodles until al dente. Drain.
  2. Cook ground beef. Add tomato sauce and paste. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Assemble layers of sauce, noodles, and ricotta cheese in a 13x9 baking dish.
  4. Base for 30 minutes at 375 degrees F.
